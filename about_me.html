Agathe Eiselé

I was born in France and I have studied both in Switzerland and in the Netherlands. I hold two master’s degrees from the University of Utrecht — one in Clinical Psychology and another in Social and Health Psychology. I still live in Utrecht where I like to cycle around, always carrying my waterproof equipment, just in case.

I am a sensitive person who is very attuned to people. Being a good listener and passionate about psychology, I naturally became a therapist. Providing mental support and guidance brings meaning to my life and I deeply value the trust that people put in me.

Growing up in the Alps, the contact with nature was an important aspect of my life and contributed to shaping who I am. Nowadays, I still seek out this connection which I like to combine with some of my hobbies: I love to climb, hike, and run in the mountains. That is my safe place and where I feel at peace.
