Frequently Asked Questions

  • There is currently no waiting time. Updated on 10/08/2024.
  • I offer therapy in English and French.
  • Sessions can take place in my practice at Gansstraat 164, 3582EP Utrecht or online.
  • During this call, we get to know each other and I ask about the reasons that led you to seek out therapy. The aim is to determine the range and severity of your complaints and estimate if I can provide help.
  • I usually see clients every week or every other week. However, the frequency of sessions is quite flexible and can be adapted depending on the severity of the complaints and preferences.
  • The therapy is tailored to each patient. What works for one person may not work for another. I use mostly techniques derived from Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Schema Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and Attachment theory.
